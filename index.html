About

Hi, my name is Tain Reid, and I’m a sound designer based in West Lothian, Scotland. I specialize in sound for film and television - covering everything from recording and dialogue editing to mixing and Foley work.

I'm currently studying Sound Design at Edinburgh Napier University, where I continue to expand my technical skills and creative approach. I have a deep passion for music and sound, and I love exploring how audio can shape and elevate storytelling.

Whether it's designing soundscapes, editing dialogue, or bringing scenes to life through Foley, I aim to create immersive audio experiences for audiences.

Far Cry 3 Sound Re-design | April 2023

Created a complete soundtrack for 3-minutes of continuous gameplay captured from a commercially released video game (Far Cry 3). All existing audio was replaced and a full set of audio assets added, contrasting the original to give a different ‘feel’.
Interactive Audio Programming 360 VR Game | Nature Scenes | April 2023
Job Roles include: Post-Production Sound Designer, Editor, ADR Mixer, Foley Artist, Dubbing Mixer, Voice Actor, Music Supervisor and Audio Programmer
Copyright free experience for a VR, 360-degree video game in Unity 3D. The C# scripting language was used to attach audio to objects within the game and trigger events. The project is aimed at people who are looking for peace and relaxation. It is a peaceful journey through different relaxing scenes in nature.
The player explores rural, nature-based locations as 3D environments. The game uses 360-degree videos from YouTube. Some scenes have multiple hotspots which are different locations within the scene which allows the player to explore more of the location they are currently in. The game is an immersive experience with 4 channel ambisonic audio to give the player a real feeling of immersion when playing. This adds realism to the sound within the game. All sounds added are copyright free.
